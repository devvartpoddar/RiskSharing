  • Risk sharing may fail in presence of credit contraints; i.e. individuals without sufficient collatral would borrow less than "well endowed" households; The author look at the interaction between assets and shocks on net borrowings

  • Risk sharing may also fail if transfers limited to closely connected individuals. Credibility of repayment, or altruism due to personal relationships. Netwrok Quality Important?

  • Risk sharing and tranfer of credit in place of indebtedness

  • Finally, authors also explore the wether altruism is a powerful motive in risk sharing, or presence of self interest in providing credit

206 Households spread across 4 villages surveyed


  • Three interviews conducted in 4 villages in rural Philippines between July 1994, (after rice harvest) and March 1995, (after new crop has been transplanted)

  • 206 households randomly selected for the interviews. Data collected include list of remitances and gifts given / recieved, income and consumtion shocks and characteristics such as cultivated land, age of head among others

  • Complete data about network partners also collected in similar detail.
